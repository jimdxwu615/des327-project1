Mechanical Skills

The first shop that I met after coming to San Francisco for university was REN Autoworks, I met the boss during a mountain cruise and we became friend later. I always go to the shop to see how to work on cars, I also work on my own car in my home with the knowledge I learn from them and Youtube.

At 2021’s summer, I became a volunteer at REN Autoworks, helping them to modify or repair cars. I really enjoyed the experience and my mechanical skills improved a lot.

Now, I’m able to a lot of car modifications and repairs by myself. These skills also helped me a lot when I’m working on my own car. Great thanks to REN Autoworks!
